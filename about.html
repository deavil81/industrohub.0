About IndustroHub

IndustroHub is an Industrial Demand Network designed to connect buyers, manufacturers, traders, and suppliers in one simple platform.

Businesses often struggle to quickly find reliable suppliers for raw materials, fabrication work, machining, and industrial services. IndustroHub solves this problem by allowing buyers to post their requirements and receive supplier offers.

How It Works

1. Post Requirement

Buyers post their industrial material or service requirement with quantity, specifications, and delivery details.

2. Suppliers View RFQs

Suppliers and manufacturers can view live RFQs posted by buyers.

3. Connect & Deal

Suppliers contact buyers directly with quotations and delivery terms.

Who Can Use IndustroHub

  • Manufacturers
  • Steel traders
  • Fabrication workshops
  • Machining service providers
  • Industrial suppliers
  • Construction contractors

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a powerful industrial sourcing network that helps small and medium businesses quickly find suppliers, reduce procurement time, and grow their operations.
